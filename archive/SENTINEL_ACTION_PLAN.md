**Probability:** Medium
**Impact:** High

**Mitigation:**
- Target 50-100 investors
- Have strong pitch materials
- Demonstrate traction (beta users)
- Show clear path to revenue
- Have contingency plan (smaller raise, bridge financing)

**Contingency Plan:**
- Raise $2-3M instead of $5M
- Extend timeline to 24 months
- Reduce team size to 10-12 people
- Focus on MVP features only

### Technical Risk
**Risk:** Hypervisor development more complex than expected
**Probability:** Medium
**Impact:** High

**Mitigation:**
- Hire experienced hypervisor developers
- Use existing hypervisor frameworks
- Start with simplified implementation
- Allocate extra time for testing

**Contingency Plan:**
- Use open-source hypervisor (KVM, Xen)
- Simplify MVP scope
- Extend timeline by 2-3 months

**Risk:** Slow market adoption
**Probability:** Medium
**Impact:** High

**Mitigation:**

- Beta testing program
- Competitive pricing
- Focus on unique advantages

**Contingency Plan:**

- Extend marketing campaign
