## 📜 Company Story

### The Problem
Traditional antivirus solutions are failing in the modern threat landscape. Operating at Ring 0 or Ring 3 of the operating system, they miss critical threats, generate high false positive rates (0.05-0.5%), consume significant system resources (1-18% CPU), and conflict with gaming anti-cheat systems. Most importantly, they offer no protection against future quantum computing attacks.

### The Solution
SENTINEL was founded on a simple premise: security should be invisible, intelligent, and future-proof. We built an AI-native platform that operates at Ring -1 (below the kernel level), providing unmatched visibility and control. Our quantum-ready cryptography protects against tomorrow's threats today, and our gaming-first design actually improves gaming performance (+21% FPS, -77% latency).

### The Mission
To revolutionize cybersecurity by making it intelligent, invisible, and future-proof, protecting billions of users and devices worldwide.

### The Vision
A world where security is seamless, intelligent, and never compromises performance or privacy.
